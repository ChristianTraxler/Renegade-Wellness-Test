Himalayan Salt

Authentic Himalayan salt lamps are natural air purifiers that come straight from the foothills of the Himalayan Mountains. This is the only place in the world where this type of salt comes from. Himalayan salt contains 84 trace minerals and the different color variations you see on each lamp are created by the different concentrations of these minerals naturally occurring in the mine. When warmed, Himalayan salt emits negative ions which purify the air and have a relaxing effect on the body. Their warm glow also helps relax the eyes from the overuse of electronic devices.
